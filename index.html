About Us

West Tennessee Healthcare is a public, not-for-profit healthcare system servicing a population of 500,000 in West Tennessee. With over 7,000 employees, West Tennessee Healthcare provides a full continuum of care including seven
hospitals, two medical centers, outpatient surgical facility, home healthcare agency, mental and behavioral health, medical laboratory services, medical products, primary care and specialty clinics, outpatient infusion services, outpatient rehab facilities, outpatient imaging, early child development intervention services, community support, and education.
